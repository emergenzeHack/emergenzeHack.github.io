emergenzeHack

We face emergencies with civic hacking, tansforming data into collective knowledge with open source software and tools to aid the population.

emergenzeHack is an inclusive collective of italian civic activists formed in 2016, when the Central Italy earthquake affected millions of lives: we created TerremotoCentroItalia to match the needs of the population and the available offers, while keeping people updated. In 2020, we are replicating this effort with Covid19Italia Help.
We are volunteers with very varied backgrounds: developers, hackers, journalists, social media managers, editors and much more.

How

We provide tools and workflows to:

Aggregate

Gather, crowd-verify, label and geolocalise data coming from forms, bots and other sources.

Open

Expose freely the resulting enriched datasets to the community, aiding similar projects.

Distribute

Present, geolocalise and deliver information to interested targets using story telling, socials and communication tools.

What's happening?

Covid19Help is serving more than 1,5 K reports. From psicological support services to local initiatives offering delivery of primary needs and medications. We geolocalise, crowd-verify and distribute these informations on maps, socials and feeds to tens of thousands visitors. Greek and Portuguese versions are work in progress.
In 2016-2019, TerremotoCentroItalia gathered 2K reports, matching people in need with solidarity initiatives.
